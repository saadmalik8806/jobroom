A Business Account requires mandatory documentation to ensure the integrity of the platform and protect both users and the company. You’ll need to provide your proof of identity and for secure verification purposes, we’ll also capture a quick facial scan using your device’s camera. This process is quick and helps ensure the security of your account and complies with regulatory requirements. You’ll also be required to provide your company’s bank details, email address, phone number, and registration documents. Our team will review them for authenticity and ensure the information provided matches your company registration details. While JOBROOMS typically does not require an in-person visit to your business to sign you up on the platform, there may be some exceptions depending on your location and specific circumstances. In some cases, a JOBROOMS representative may contact you for a phone call or video chat to discuss your application and answer any questions you may have. However, a physical visit to your business is not a standard part of the process. The onboarding process typically takes approximately 5 minutes, and upon successful account approval, typically within 24 hours, you’ll unlock the comprehensive benefits of a JOBROOMS account, this means you’ll be able to:
  • Embrace a seamless system that delivers free and consistent customer leads directly to your business. Our innovative approach bypasses the complexities of advertising, whether you operate a dental care clinic, an accounting firm, or a cleaning company, a Business Account will make it easier for customers to find you. And with JOBROOMS’ comprehensive onboarding process, customers know they’re connecting with a registered, vetted, and insured business they can trust. Ditch ineffective paid ads and stop cold calling prospects and worrying about were your next client is going to come from. Instead, focus on the question that truly matters: How can you deliver exceptional value to your customers? JOBROOMS will take care of finding your next clients, all for free. Your only job is to accept help request calls, take shopping lists, job offers, and B2B contracts.
  • Enjoy the flexibility to meet seasonal or temporary staffing needs. JOBROOMS offers businesses the flexibility of “switching-on and switching-off” recruitment solutions as recruitment needs are often unpredictable or sporadic. As a business owner, you’ll be able to adapt your hiring and staffing needs as your business demands fluctuate, ensuring optimal resource allocation.
  • Optimize your resource utilization and deliver cost-effective labor and delivery solutions. As a business owner, you’ll be able to minimize expenses by eliminating the need for recruitment agencies and fleet ownership. A Business Account will allow you to instantly connect with highly vetted workers to complete tasks on-site or remotely, at a fraction of the cost of traditional recruitment agencies. You’ll be able to choose from a pool of thoroughly screened and qualified workers available whenever you need them to fulfil your business needs.
  • Embrace digital time sheets and management. A Business Account will allow you to streamline your workflow with digital time sheets and efficient management of critical documents like employee records, invoices, contracts, and more, securing you from unwanted changes so you can pay exactly what you need and nothing more.
  • Eliminate the need to constantly switch between platforms, manage multiple accounts, and juggle different subscription fees. A Business Account will allow you to order and receive almost anything anytime, from hair, beauty, and wellness services, to live coaching and tutoring, all conveniently accessible on one platform.
  • Use JOBROOMS’ innovative ‘’Panic and Neighbourhood Watch’’ feature to connect instantly with local authorities and community members during emergencies or danger situations, ensuring your safety and well-being at all times.
  • Connect with qualified professionals for emotional support, financial assistance, or professional guidance. At JOBROOMS we know that life can be challenging, so if you’re facing any difficulties, we’re here to listen and help.
  • Say goodbye to traditional consultations! JOBROOMS puts a team of qualified experts at your fingertips, ready to help you tackle your daily needs with personalized advice and support, all via convenient live audio/ video calls.
  • Experience AI on a new level! JOBROOMS’ chat assistant delivers human-quality answers to your questions, providing instant support and information like a friendly and knowledgeable friend. Unleash the power of our generative AI tool to explore a vast realm of knowledge and support.
  • Seamlessly purchase goods and services directly through the platform. Skip the aisles and experience instant, personalized shopping! Simply submit your shopping list and you’ll be connected with a dedicated personal shopper via video chat, ready to curate your perfect finds.

A Partial Access Account (PAA) is swift and only requires your email address and basic bank information for secure transactions. A PAA offers access to crucial benefits, including free emergency help and instant face-to-face interactions with qualified human experts who can delve deep into your queries, understand your unique needs, and offer tailored guidance in real-time, or chat with an AI agent capable of generating human-quality responses. Additionally, a PAA allows convenient online shopping, enabling you to seamlessly purchase goods and services directly through the platform. At JOBROOMS we know that life can be challenging. If you’re facing any difficulties, we’re here to listen and help. We can instantly connect you with qualified professionals for emotional support, financial assistance, or professional guidance. Use JOBROOMS’ innovative ‘’Panic and Neighbourhood Watch’’ feature to connect instantly with local authorities and community members during emergencies or danger situations, ensuring your safety and well-being at all times.

However, it’s important to note that you cannot generate income or gain any financial benefits with a ‘’Partial Access Account’’. This means you cannot establish connections with potential clients, accept help request calls, take shopping lists, job offers, and B2B contracts. If you attempt to access either of these restricted features, you will be prompted to create a Personal or Business account. To enhance the safety of both users and service providers, we’ve restricted accounts with incomplete information. This means that users with Partial Access Accounts can access our shopping feature only between 8am and 6pm. This is due to limited account information, which may hinder verification in case of incidents. Additionally, Partial Access Accounts do not qualify for the £5 welcome credit for the first consultation call.
